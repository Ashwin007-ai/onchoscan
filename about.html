About OnchoScan

OnchoScan is an AI-assisted cancer screening platform using deep learning CNNs to detect brain tumors and skin cancer from medical images. Provides confidence scores, risk assessments, Grad-CAM heatmaps, and clinical recommendations in seconds.

🤖

AI Models

Brain TumorResNet-18 · 4 classesGlioma · Meningioma · No Tumor · Pituitary
Skin CancerResNet-18 · 2 classesBenign · Malignant
📊

Datasets

Brain MRI Dataset

Multi-class tumor MRI dataset with glioma, meningioma, pituitary and healthy scans.

HAM10000

10,000+ dermatoscopic skin lesion images for binary classification.

🔬

Explainability (Grad-CAM)

Gradient-weighted Class Activation Mapping highlights which image regions influenced the model's decision, making AI interpretable for clinical review.
